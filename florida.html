Places to Visit

Miami Beach

Miami Beach is an absolute must if you will be road tripping down the through the South Beach! Miami beach is known for its great entertainment and sensational crystal blue waters. You will not be dissapointed when you hit these shores...

Wynwood

Interested in some cool modern street art? Well Wynwood Art District is just the place for you. The walls on the streets are filled with beautiful street murals that create a very colorful and vibrant scene.

Little Havana

Little Havana is a great hub dedicated to the Hispanic culture within South Florida. With some great Latin inspired music, food, and shops, this is definitely a place you need to stop at on your road trip!

Sports

Basketball

Stop by and see some dunks and hoops when the Miami Heat play at American Airlines Areana!

Football

Watch some touchdowns and dudes hitting hitting each other when the Dolphins play at Hard Rock Stadium!

Baseball

Catch some foul balls when the Miami Marlins play at Marlins Park!

Activities

The Everglades

Catch up with some cool alligators and other swap animals in world famous Everglades National Park!

Night Clubs

Hit the clubs with your friends if you plan on staying for a while on your trip!

Kayaking

You and your friends can cool down and go kayaking around the city!
